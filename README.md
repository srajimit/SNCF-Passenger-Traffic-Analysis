# SNCF-Passenger-Traffic-Analysis
An interactive Excel dashboard analysing 10 years of passenger  traffic across 3,000+ French railway stations (2015-2024),  focusing on COVID-19 impact and regional recovery patterns.

## Business Question
Which French regions and stations drove passenger traffic recovery post-COVID, and where has traffic still not returned to 2019 levels?

## Key Findings
- SNCF national traffic reached 3,068.3M passengers in 2024
- National recovery rate: 112.27% of pre-COVID 2019 levels
- COVID-19 caused -47.72% collapse in 2020
- All 8 SNCF regions exceeded pre-COVID levels by 2024
- Île-de-France (DEX GIF) accounts for 50% of national traffic
- Year-on-year growth of 10% in 2024 shows continued momentum

## Dashboard Features
- 4 Interactive KPI Cards
- 10-year passenger trend line chart with COVID annotation
- Regional traffic comparison bar chart
- COVID recovery rate by region (conditional colour coding)
- Top 10 busiest stations ranking
- Traffic by station segment donut chart
- Interactive slicers for Region and Station Type filtering

  ## Tools Used
- Microsoft Excel
- Pivot Tables and Pivot Charts

- Slicers and Timeline
- Conditional Formatting
- Custom Chart Formatting

## Data Source
Official SNCF Open Data — ressources.data.sncf.com
